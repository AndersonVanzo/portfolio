About me

Hi, I’m Anderson Vanzo, a passionate and results-driven Mobile and Web Developer with a knack for creating seamless, user-centric applications. With over 4 of experience in mobile and full-stack development, I specialize in building robust, scalable, and innovative solutions that enhance user experiences and drive business success.

My journey in tech began with a degree in Internet Computing from the Catarinense Federal Institute, where I developed a strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and web development. Since then, I’ve honed my skills in technologies like React Native, TypeScript, Kotlin, Java, Swift, and Node.js, and have become adept at integrating cutting-edge tools and frameworks to deliver high-quality applications.

I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy mentoring new team members, sharing knowledge, and contributing to a culture of continuous improvement. Whether it’s optimizing workflows, solving complex technical challenges, or crafting engaging user interfaces, I’m always driven by a desire to create meaningful and innovative solutions.
